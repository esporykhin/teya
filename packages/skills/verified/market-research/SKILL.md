# Market Research

Use this skill when the user needs structured market analysis before launching, pricing, positioning, or prioritizing a product.

## Workflow

1. Define the target market, segment, and geography.
2. Identify direct alternatives and substitute solutions.
3. Compare pricing, positioning, feature depth, and distribution.
4. Summarize demand signals, constraints, and open questions.
5. End with a concrete recommendation, not just raw research.

## Output

- Market snapshot
- Top competitors and how they differ
- Pricing and packaging patterns
- Risks, gaps, and next actions
